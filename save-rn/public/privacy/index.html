SAV-E Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

SAV-E is a private place-memory app. This policy explains what we collect, why, and the choices you have. SAV-E is built to be private by default: your saved places are yours, and there is no public feed.

What We Collect

Account. When you sign in with Apple, Google, or email through our authentication provider, Privy, we receive an account identifier and, depending on the method, your email address. We use this to create and secure your account.

Your saved content. Places you save, review candidates, notes, source links, and reviews you write are stored in our backend so your map can sync across your devices.

Location. With your permission, SAV-E uses your device location to show nearby places and answer "what is near me" questions. We do not sell your location or use it to build an advertising profile.

Search text. When you ask SAV-E a question or paste a link, that text may be sent to place and AI providers, including Google Places and Google Gemini, to identify places or answer your query.

We do not collect contacts, browsing history, or advertising identifiers, and SAV-E has no ads.

How We Use Data

  • To save, find, confirm, and search your places.
  • To sync your private map across your devices.
  • To keep your account secure.

We do not sell your personal data, and we do not show your saved places to other users or post them publicly. Sharing only happens when you explicitly create a shareable link.

Third-Party Services

SAV-E uses Privy for sign-in, Supabase for backend storage, Google Places for place resolution, Google Gemini for AI answers, and Apple or Google Maps when you choose to open directions.

Data Retention and Deletion

Your saved content stays until you delete it. You can delete individual places in the app. To delete your account and associated data, contact us at support@sav-e.app; we will remove it within 30 days.

Children

SAV-E is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

Contact

Questions or deletion requests: support@sav-e.app
